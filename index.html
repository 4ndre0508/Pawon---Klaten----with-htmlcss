ABOUT US

Pawon Klaten is your one-stop destination for traditional culinary delights from Klaten. We pride ourselves in offering a wide range of authentic and flavorful dishes that are rich in spices and embody the true essence of Klaten's culinary heritage.

At Pawon Klaten, we understand the importance of preserving the traditional flavors and recipes that have been passed down through generations. Our team of skilled and passionate chefs meticulously prepare each dish using only the finest ingredients sourced locally to ensure an authentic taste experience with every bite.

We offer an extensive selection of traditional Klaten dishes, ranging from nasi liwet, gudeg, sate klathak, to selat solo. Each dish is expertly crafted to perfection, highlighting the unique blend of spices that make Klaten cuisine truly special.

Customer satisfaction is our utmost priority. We strive to provide excellent customer service, ensuring that every visit to Pawon Klaten is an enjoyable and memorable one. Our friendly staff are always ready to assist and guide you in choosing the perfect meal to satisfy your cravings.

Pawon Klaten also caters to special events and gatherings.
